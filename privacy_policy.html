Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Wani Wallet ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the application.

1. Overview

Wani Wallet is a personal finance tracking application that analyzes financial SMS messages to provide insights into your spending and income.

The app is intended for users located in Malawi.

2. SMS Access and Processing

a. Permission (READ_SMS)

With your explicit consent, Wani Wallet accesses SMS messages on your device to identify financial transaction messages such as bank alerts and mobile money notifications.

b. Limited Scope

The app only processes SMS messages that match financial transaction patterns.

We do NOT read, store, or process personal or non-financial messages.

c. Extracted Data

The app may extract the following from relevant messages:

  • Transaction amount
  • Transaction type
  • Date and time
  • Sender name

3. Local Processing Guarantee

All SMS content and extracted financial data are processed and stored locally on your device.

SMS content is never transmitted to external servers, including analytics platforms such as Firebase.

4. Analytics and Firebase Usage

We use analytics tools (such as Firebase Analytics) to collect limited, non-personal usage data to improve app performance and user experience.

This data may include:

  • App usage events (e.g., feature usage)
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Device-level performance data

The following data is NOT collected, transmitted, or shared with Firebase or any analytics service:

  • SMS content
  • Transaction details
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Personally identifiable financial data

5. Personal Data

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or email address.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties.

7. Future Advertising

Wani Wallet currently does not display advertisements.

We reserve the right to introduce advertising in the future. If implemented, this policy will be updated accordingly.

8. User Control

You can control app permissions at any time through your device settings.

You may revoke SMS access at any time, though some features may stop working.

9. Data Retention

All data remains on your device and can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data within the application.

11. Compliance with Malawi Data Protection Principles

We adhere to key data protection principles aligned with the laws of Malawi, including:

  • Lawful processing based on user consent
  • Purpose limitation
  • Data minimization
  • Security safeguards

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent by revoking permissions
  • Delete your data locally

12. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
